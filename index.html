ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tire, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range Technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee ahop and America Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TOPTRACER RANGE

Our range drives the same ball-tracking techmology that traces the hsots of the game's best players on TV. Toptracer Range technology offers a fun, engaging, tech-driven experience that appeals to seasoned players, range rivals, friends, family members, and even first-time golfers.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Adventure awaits you! Become a jurassic explorer as you delve into the land of the dinosaurs! Putt your way through prehistoric landscape, cascading waterfalls and meet some dinosaur friends along the way.
